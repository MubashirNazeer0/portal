Q1: What are the conditions for field registration?

Q2: What is the required rate for the field?

A: A GPA of 2.2 and above is required.

Q3: How many units are needed to register for the field?

A: 90 units and above.

Q4: How many hours is the field training?

A: 4 hours a day.

Q5: What are the field training days for the first and second semesters?

A: Sunday, Tuesday, and Thursday.

Q6: What are the field training days in the summer semester?

A: All days of the week.

Q7: How do I update my profile information?

A: Log in to the alumni portal and navigate to the "Profile" section. Click "Edit Profile" to update your information, such as your contact details, work experience, or educational background.

Q8: Are there webinars or skill-building sessions?

A: Yes, the alumni portal regularly hosts webinars and workshops focused on professional development. Check the "Events" section or the announcements page for a list of upcoming sessions.

Q9: Can I access the portal if I graduated a long time ago?

A: Yes, the portal is open to all alumni, regardless of when you graduated. If you face issues accessing the platform, contact the support team for assistance.

Q10: Is the portal only for alumni from specific programs or years?

A: No, the alumni portal is designed to serve all alumni from various programs, departments, and graduating years.

Q11: How do I find upcoming alumni events?

A: Visit the "Events" tab on the portal. You can view the event calendar, RSVP, and access details about alumni meetups, webinars, or reunions.

Q12: How do I update my contact information or profile details?

A: Access the "Settings" or "Profile" page and use the available fields to update your contact details. Be sure to save your changes to keep your profile current.

Q13: How do I manage my privacy and notification settings?

A: Go to the "Settings" section in your account. From there, adjust your privacy preferences, such as who can see your profile, and customize your notification settings for emails or updates.

Q14: What should I do if I encounter issues logging in?

A: Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset your credentials. If the issue persists, contact technical support for further assistance.

Q15: Is there a mobile app for the alumni network?

A: No, a mobile app is unavailable for convenient access to the alumni portal. You can only use the portal for now.

Q16: How can I suggest improvements or new features for the portal?

A: Use the "Feedback" option on the portal or email the support team directly. Your suggestions are valuable and help us enhance the alumni experience.
